## Before Contacting Support

To help us assist you more effectively, please:

### Check Our Resources
1. **[FAQ](faq.md)** - Common questions and solutions
2. **[Troubleshooting Guide](troubleshooting.md)** - Step-by-step problem solving
3. **[User Manual](../user-guide/user-manual.md)** - Comprehensive user guide

### Gather Information
When contacting support, please include:
- **Account email address**
- **Detailed description** of the issue
- **Steps to reproduce** the problem
- **Error messages** (exact text or screenshots)
- **Browser and operating system** information
- **What you've already tried** to resolve the issue

### Escalation Process
If your issue isn't resolved within the expected timeframe:
1. **Reply to your ticket** requesting escalation
2. **Call the support line** and reference your ticket number
3. **Contact your account manager** (Enterprise customers)
4. **Email escalations@smartcomex.com** for urgent matters
