# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about SmartComex below. If you can't find what you're looking for, please [contact our support team](contact.md).

## General Questions

### What is SmartComex?
SmartComex is a comprehensive e-commerce platform that helps businesses manage their online stores, process orders, track inventory, and analyze performance. It's designed to scale from small businesses to enterprise-level operations.

### Is SmartComex suitable for my business size?
Yes! SmartComex offers plans for businesses of all sizes:
- **Starter**: Perfect for new businesses and small stores
- **Professional**: Ideal for growing businesses
- **Enterprise**: Designed for large-scale operations

### What's the difference between cloud and on-premises deployment?
- **Cloud**: Hosted by SmartComex, automatic updates, no maintenance required
- **On-premises**: Installed on your servers, full control, requires technical expertise

### Can I migrate from another e-commerce platform?
Yes, we provide migration tools and services for popular platforms including:
- Shopify
- WooCommerce
- Magento
- BigCommerce
- Custom solutions

## Troubleshooting

### Why can't I log in?
Common solutions:
1. Check your username and password
2. Ensure caps lock is off
3. Try resetting your password
4. Clear browser cache
5. Contact support if issues persist

### Why aren't my emails working?
Check these settings:
1. SMTP configuration
2. Email templates
3. Spam folder
4. Domain reputation
5. Email service provider status

### My orders aren't processing correctly
Verify:
1. Payment gateway configuration
2. Inventory levels
3. Shipping settings
4. Tax configuration
5. Order workflow settings

### Performance seems slow
Try these steps:
1. Clear browser cache
2. Check internet connection
3. Optimize images
4. Review installed apps
5. Contact support for analysis
